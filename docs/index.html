How It Works

Step 1

Scan the QR Code

The TV displays a QR code and 4-digit PIN while idle. Guests scan with any phone — no app needed.

Step 2

Enter the PIN

The first person to enter the PIN becomes the controller for this round.

Step 3

Pick a Video

Browse the grid and choose a title. Once you pick, it plays on the big screen immediately.

Step 4

Watch Together

The video plays to completion. Others see a now-playing screen with poster art. A fresh PIN is issued for the next round.

Features

No App Required

Everything runs in the phone's browser. Guests just scan and go.

Choose Wisely

Videos play to the end in normal mode — no one can stop someone else's pick.

Free Play Mode

Skip the PIN entirely. Anyone can pick or stop videos — great for personal use or relaxed environments.

Admin Panel

Force-reset, play any video, reboot the Pi, and toggle modes — all from a password-protected web page.

Easy Content Management

Drop a video file and a poster image into a named folder under media/. That's it.

Runs Locally

No cloud, no subscriptions, no internet required. Everything stays on your local network.
